Introduction

Background

I took my first plane ride before I took my first steps.

Each place gave me the opportunity to learn from many different cultures and traditions, as well as people from all walks of life.

In fact, it is travelling the world which also greatly shaped my path towards neuroscience.

Let me take you through my life of travel, showing you the beautiy of each place and my favorite thing about it.

Travel Timeline

Montenegro

2002 to 2003

The place where I was born, and only lived for 8 months before moving away.

Montenegro is known for its nature, including its beautiful coast and mountain ranges.

My favorite place is the old town of Kotor, with its beautiful cultural scene along with the breathtaking nature and water.

Italy

2003 to 2009

The place I started kindergarden and later came back to finish highschool.

Italy is known for its amazing culture and history which you can see as you stroll down any street.

My favorite memories of Italy were likely the yearly carnivals, full of life and color.

Philippines

2010 to 2015

The place I learned English!

The Philippines are known for its wildlife, with so many natural treasures found all around

My favorite memories of the Philippines are of my family and I scuba diving!

Italy

2015 to 2020

I went back to Italy to finish highschool!

This probably marked some of the best years of my life.

It is also when I first went to Amalfi coast, which is now likely my favorite place on this planet, with such beautiful scenery, beaches, and amazing food.

Florida

2020 to 2024

I moved to Miami to start my Undergraduate career!

As a now graduating senior, I can look back at these 4 years with a lot of gratitude, meeting so many amazing friends and gaining so many valuable experiences.

My favorite place in Florida is likely any beach, just spending a relaxing weekend with friends

Next Stop

2024 to...

I will be moving to Chicago in the fall to start my PhD in Neuroscience.

I am excited to find out what my favorite thing about this new place will be!
